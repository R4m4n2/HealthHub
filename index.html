Weekly Diet Plan - No Carbs Diet

Day 1

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and avocado

Lunch: Grilled chicken with broccoli and mixed greens

Dinner: Baked salmon with asparagus and a side salad

Day 2

Breakfast: Omelette with mushrooms, bell peppers, and cheese

Lunch: Tuna salad with lettuce, cucumber, and olive oil dressing

Dinner: Grilled shrimp with steamed cauliflower and broccoli

Day 3

Breakfast: Greek yogurt with berries and nuts

Lunch: Grilled turkey with zucchini noodles and tomato sauce

Dinner: Baked chicken with Brussels sprouts and a side salad

Day 4

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and feta cheese

Lunch: Baked cod with asparagus and quinoa

Dinner: Grilled steak with roasted cauliflower and a side salad

Day 5

Breakfast: Omelette with spinach, mushrooms, and goat cheese

Lunch: Tofu stir-fry with broccoli, bell peppers, and soy sauce

Dinner: Baked trout with green beans and a side salad

Day 6

Breakfast: Greek yogurt with sliced almonds and honey

Lunch: Grilled chicken with asparagus and a side salad

Dinner: Baked salmon with Brussels sprouts and quinoa

Day 7

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with tomatoes and basil

Lunch: Turkey and avocado lettuce wraps

Dinner: Grilled shrimp with cauliflower rice and a side salad
